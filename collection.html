Brief Information Of Our Collection

      The major role of a National Library is to preserve and expand the use of published collection from within a respective country as well as collection published in other countries that relates to it. As the National Library for Malaysia, this institution is the central repository for Malaysia’s historical memory and culture as well as a library that stores, documents and conserve intellectual achievement that has been translated into print and non-print form. The Malaysiana collection is a collection of national heritage and the nation of Malaysia.

Monographs are books covering all subject fields such as General Works, Philosophy, Religion, Language, Fundamental Science, Applied Science, Social Science, Technology, Arts, Literature, Geography,History and Biography.

Malaysiana Monograph collection is located on Level 7 and 8 , PNM building. This collection comprises of materials published in Malaysia and/or about Malaysia published in other countries and/or produced by Malaysian citizen regardless of shape,language, script or date generated. Symbols used to identify this collection is M (Malaysiana), MR (Malaysiana Reference), MB (Malaysiana Brochure), f M (Malaysiana folio) and ff M (Malaysiana doubles folio).

General Monograph Collection is a major collection available at the General Information Section of PNM. This collection constitute foreign issues of reference monographs such as books, directories, dictionaries, encyclopedias, manuals, atlas, almanacs, thesaurus and Multimedia Reference Kit (RMK) from various subjects. The general monograph collection is located at the Centre Block on Level 3 of PNM Building.

Thus, there are other collection such as serial, conference paper, newspaper, map, electronic media collections, Malay manuscripts, virtual exhibition, PNM digital collection, Wacana Manuskrip Melayu and e-proceeding. Meanwhile under special collections there 8 categories which are personal, rare, ephemeral, limited / classified / banned, Harvard, United Nations, Kazakhstan and lastly archive collection.
